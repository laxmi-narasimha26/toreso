## Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**Risk**: Technology Stack Changes
- **Mitigation**: Lock down stack early, avoid mid-project changes

**Risk**: Integration Challenges
- **Mitigation**: Early proof-of-concepts, vendor support agreements

**Risk**: Performance Issues
- **Mitigation**: Regular performance testing, optimization cycles

### Resource Risks

**Risk**: Key Team Member Departure
- **Mitigation**: Documentation, knowledge sharing, backup resources

**Risk**: Budget Overrun
- **Mitigation**: Regular budget reviews, contingency fund, prioritization

### Timeline Risks

**Risk**: Scope Creep
- **Mitigation**: Strict change control process, backlog management

**Risk**: Dependencies Delays
- **Mitigation**: Parallel workstreams, early integration, regular sync-ups
