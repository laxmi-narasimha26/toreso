# Feature — Pricing, Instant Offers, Volume Discounts (Spec: Goals 3, 5)

Scope
- Transparent pricing, instant offers, consolidated volumes across plants for better tiers

Capabilities
- Rules engine for tiered pricing; cross-plant volume consolidation; enterprise pricing profiles
- Price alerts; dynamic pricing support for suppliers

Acceptance Criteria
- When buyer adds items from multiple plants, Then volume tiers apply across combined quantities if policy allows
- Price alert triggers notification on threshold changes

Dependencies
- Pricing service; notifications; contracts
