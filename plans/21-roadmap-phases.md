# Roadmap & Phases (mapped to spec)

Phase 1 — Planning & Development (Months 1–8)
- Market research, business model, legal/regulatory, platform foundation

Phase 2 — Launch Preparation (Months 8–12)
- Supplier onboarding/verification, buyer education, marketing, support/training

Phase 3 — Launch (Month 12–15)
- Launch campaign, initial acquisition, feedback loop

Phase 4 — Growth & Optimization (Month 15–24)
- Innovation cadence, partnerships, user growth & engagement, finance/logistics optimization

Phase 5 — Scaling & Global Expansion (Month 24–36)
- Scalability plan execution, localization/regulatory adaptation, performance hardening

Phase 6 — Continued Growth & Sustainability (Ongoing)
- Profitability focus, industry leadership, new features & diversification
