# Project Overview - Toreso B2B Packaging Marketplace

## Executive Summary

Toreso is an enterprise-grade B2B e-marketplace revolutionizing the packaging materials industry by connecting buyers (medium and small-scale industries) with verified suppliers. The platform aims to minimize operational costs, provide transparent pricing, optimize procurement processes, and standardize quality across the packaging supply chain.

## Vision

To become the leading global B2B marketplace for packaging materials, setting industry standards for transparency, quality, efficiency, and sustainability in packaging procurement.

## Mission

Empowering medium and small-scale industries to expand their vision and reach potential buyers while exploring new opportunities through education, training, and process standardization.

## Problem Statement

### Buyer Pain Points
1. **High Procurement Costs** - Excessive spending on packaging materials without cost control
2. **Inventory Management Issues** - Overstocking and inefficient Just-In-Time (JIT) deliveries
3. **Lack of Trusted Partners** - Difficulty finding reliable, quality-assured suppliers
4. **Price Opacity** - No single price point or transparent pricing mechanism
5. **Wrong Procurement Decisions** - Insufficient data for informed purchasing
6. **Delivery Delays** - Poor control over supplier delivery timelines
7. **Quality Inconsistencies** - Lack of quality assurance and standardization
8. **Transparency Issues** - Lack of commitment and transparency from suppliers
9. **Fragmented Purchasing** - Unable to consolidate packaging purchases across plants
10. **Plant-Level Control** - Difficulty managing purchases across multiple plant locations
11. **Audit Bypassing** - Systems and audits being circumvented
12. **Review Complexity** - Difficult to review packaging purchases at plant level
13. **Performance Tracking** - Hard to review and track supplier performance
14. **Payment Follow-ups** - Constant supplier follow-ups for payments

### Supplier Pain Points
1. **Limited Market Reach** - Difficulty accessing buyers beyond local networks
2. **Cash Flow Issues** - Delayed payments affecting operational liquidity
3. **Market Visibility** - Limited exposure to potential buyers
4. **Capability Assessment** - No standardized way to showcase capabilities
5. **Quality Standardization** - Lack of uniform quality standards
6. **Order Volatility** - Irregular order flow affecting production planning
7. **Audit Burden** - Complex, non-standardized audit processes
8. **Certification Management** - Difficulty tracking and renewing certifications
9. **Price Competition** - Raw material price differences causing competitive disadvantages
10. **Technology Gap** - Limited access to modern procurement technologies
