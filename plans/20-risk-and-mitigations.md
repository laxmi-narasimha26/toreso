# Risk & Mitigations (from spec Bottlenecks)

- Supplier onboarding & verification scale → automated KYC, SLA’d manual review, phased invites, dashboards
- User adoption & engagement → guided onboarding, education hub, in-app tours, ROI calculators
- Technical robustness → staged rollouts, chaos testing, SLOs, capacity planning
- Marketplace trust → verified badges, moderated reviews, dispute resolution, transparent SLAs
- Educational barriers → webinars, tutorials, certification paths, contextual help
- System integrations → API-first, integration templates, sandboxes, support SLAs
