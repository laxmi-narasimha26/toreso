# Integrations

Payments
- Gateways: Stripe/Razorpay (by geo), support cards, net-banking, UPI, bank transfer
- Credit terms via fintech partners (invoice discounting providers)

Logistics
- Carrier aggregators (Shiprocket/Easypost/Delhivery/DHL/FedEx) for labels & tracking
- Webhooks for status updates; mapping to internal Shipment model

Messaging & Notifications
- Email (SendGrid/Postmark), SMS/WhatsApp (Twilio), Push (FCM/WebPush)

Search & Analytics
- OpenSearch/Elasticsearch for catalog/search
- GA4 + server-side events; optional Segment/CDP

Blockchain (Phase 3+)
- Hash audit trails on-chain; smart contracts for select deals

AR & Sustainability (Phase 4+)
- AR viewers for supported devices; sustainability data providers (LCAs)
