# Toreso - Project Overview

## Executive Summary

Toreso is a comprehensive B2B e-marketplace platform designed to revolutionize the packaging materials industry by connecting buyers (medium and small-scale industries) with verified suppliers through a transparent, efficient, and technology-driven platform.

## Core Vision

Empower medium and small-scale industries to expand their vision, reach potential buyers, and explore new opportunities by educating, training, and standardizing their processes and work culture.

## Platform Type
- B2B E-commerce Marketplace
- Multi-vendor Platform
- Procurement Management System
- Supply Chain Optimization Platform
